About Me

I'm a Technical Consultant with over 4 years of expertise in Big Data Analytics and Intelligence through Splunk. My passion lies in transforming complex datasets into compelling visualizations and actionable insights that drive strategic business decisions.

Throughout my career, I've been highly proficient with Splunk for Data Onboarding, ETL processes, and Data Optimization. I've had the privilege of working with prestigious organizations including the NHS, Lloyds Bank, and various FinTech companies, delivering solutions that significantly impact their operational efficiency and strategic planning.

Currently, I'm pivoting toward a broader Data Analyst role by expanding my proficiency in SQL, Python, Statistics, and Tableau. This evolution allows me to offer a more comprehensive approach to data analysis and visualization across different platforms and methodologies.

I'm passionate about understanding data at its core and helping organizations leverage their data assets for strategic value and digital transformation. Whether it's implementing monitoring solutions for nationwide healthcare initiatives or developing security analytics for financial institutions, I thrive on turning data challenges into opportunities for growth and improvement.
